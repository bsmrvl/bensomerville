Manna - Live from the Living Room!
This week I accomplished a miraculously low amount of anything. Lots of grand colorful ideas and goals have been swimming around in my head like fish. But it feels like I'm the one stuck in the tank, looking out, hands on the glass–unable to truly appreciate the beauties or share them with anybody due to my confinement. That sounds way sadder than I intended; the reality is simply that I did a lot of procrastination. 

But I did manage to record the above performance of Manna, the song discussed last week (click here if you missed the history)! Little by little I'm getting comfortable on the piano, my current instrument of choice but also one of my weakest. But it's coming along! Give it a listen, because it's all I've got.

More next time (significantly..),
-Ben

SORENTINO - A Very Short Jam
Sometimes you write about what you've done and other times you just do more stuff. I was gonna throw together a quick jam and write some accompanying thoughts of the week, but it ended up taking so long to record (I probably did 60 takes of that keys track) that once again, all you get is stuff!

It's a fun one tho, and very short. Enjoy the drums-played-by-keyboard and the increasingly ridiculous video editing, not to mention the delicious overall smoothness! 

-Ben

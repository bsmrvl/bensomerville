NONSTOP TOIL - SELF JAM
I used to have fun in my home studio, played whatever came to mind on my many instruments, no thoughts of fame or career or rent. My ideas grew like mushrooms!

But for years now I haven't been able to enter that mode. All I can think about is "the next album" or "building the following". Come on! Why can't I ever mess around and let things happen on their own?

But I can! Watch the video above to behold my return to the classic Ben Somerville speed record -- no forethought, just playing the first thing that comes, and again on the next instrument, and the next. It's a lot of fun even if not quite radio ready. And don't get me wrong - it spanks. If you don't feel like scrolling up 10 lines, here it is again.

Stay safe folks! And forward this to your funky friends!
